      <h2>🎓 Sai Sudeep Das</h2>
      <p><strong>Doctoral Researcher in Computational Linguistics</strong> at IIT Delhi | Former Language Team Lead at AI4Bharat, IIT Madras</p>

    <h3>🔬 Research Journey</h3>
    <p class="justified-text">
      I am a doctoral researcher in computational linguistics at IIT Delhi. My work focuses on developing NLP methods for low-resource languages, with particular attention to Odia. I am interested in building linguistically informed models that are both theoretically grounded and practically useful.
    </p>

    <p class="justified-text">
      My academic background spans applied linguistics, language technology, and classical music. I completed my Master's degrees in Applied Linguistics and Hindustani Classical Music at the University of Hyderabad, receiving Gold Medals in both programs. These experiences have shaped the way I approach research—combining analytical rigor with close attention to sound, structure, and expression.
    </p>

    <h3>🎯 Research Philosophy</h3>
    <p class="justified-text">
      I am motivated by the goal of making language technology more inclusive and accessible. My work seeks to contribute to the preservation and development of regional languages through computational methods, while maintaining sensitivity to linguistic diversity and community needs.
    </p>

    <h3>🎵 The Music Connection</h3>
    <p class="justified-text">
      My training in classical music informs my interest in phonetics, speech processing, and acoustic analysis. Studying vocal production, rhythm, and tonal structure has deepened my engagement with questions related to sound and linguistic form. I am also interested in computational approaches to music and voice research.
    </p>

    <h3>💡 Current Focus</h3>
    <ul class="justified-text">
      <li><strong>Computational Syntax:</strong> Parsing and structural modeling for Odia</li>
      <li><strong>Data Quality Estimation:</strong> Evaluation and validation of linguistic corpora</li>
      <li><strong>Vocal Acoustics:</strong> Acoustic analysis of genre-specific singing traditions</li>
      <li><strong>Low-Resource NLP:</strong> Resource creation and modeling for underrepresented languages</li>
    </ul>

      <li>👨‍🏫 Teaching and mentoring students</li>
      <li>📸 Working on creative photo and video projects</li>
    </ul>

    <h3>🎯 Vision</h3>
    <p class="justified-text">
      I hope to contribute to a future where language technologies support linguistic diversity and reduce access barriers to knowledge. Through research and collaboration, I aim to develop tools that are both technically sound and socially meaningful.
    </p>
