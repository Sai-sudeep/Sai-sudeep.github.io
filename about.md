    <h3>🔬 Research Journey</h3>
    <p class="justified-text">I am a passionate researcher specializing in computational linguistics with a unique interdisciplinary background combining language technology and music. Currently pursuing my PhD at IIT Delhi, I focus on developing NLP solutions for low-resource languages, particularly Odia.</p>

    <p class="justified-text">My journey spans multiple domains - from linguistic analysis and AI development to classical music performance. As a Gold Medalist in both Applied Linguistics and Hindustani Classical Music from the University of Hyderabad, I bring a rare perspective that bridges technical precision with artistic sensitivity.</p>

    <h3>🎯 Research Philosophy</h3>
    <p class="justified-text">I believe in making language technology accessible and inclusive, working towards preserving and promoting regional languages through computational methods. My work aims to bridge the gap between traditional linguistic knowledge and modern AI applications.</p>

    <h3>🎵 The Music Connection</h3>
    <p class="justified-text">My dual expertise in linguistics and music isn't just academic—it's deeply interconnected. Understanding vocal acoustics, rhythm patterns, and tonal structures in classical music has enhanced my approach to phonetic research and speech processing. This unique combination allows me to explore computational musicology alongside traditional NLP research.</p>

    <h3>💡 Current Focus</h3>
    <ul class="justified-text">
      <li><strong>Computational Syntax:</strong> Developing parsing models for Odia language</li>
      <li><strong>Data Quality Estimation:</strong> Creating validation methods for linguistic corpora</li>
      <li><strong>Vocal Acoustics:</strong> Investigating genre-specific singing in Indian classical music</li>
      <li><strong>Low-Resource NLP:</strong> Building tools and resources for underrepresented languages</li>
    </ul>

    <h3>🌟 Beyond Research</h3>

      <li>📚 Contributing to open-source language resources</li>
      <li>👨‍🏫 Teaching and mentoring (former English language instructor)</li>
      <li>📸 Photo and video editing for creative projects</li>
    </ul>

    <h3>🎯 Vision</h3>
    <p class="justified-text">I envision a future where technology preserves and celebrates linguistic diversity while making knowledge accessible across language barriers. Through my work, I aim to contribute to this vision by developing inclusive AI solutions that respect and enhance our rich linguistic heritage.</p>
