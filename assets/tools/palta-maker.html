📘 How to Use This Tool(इस टूल का उपयोग कैसे करें)
+

1. Select or Enter a Scale(स्केल चुनें या दर्ज करें)

Choose a preset raga or manually enter the Aaroh (ascending) and Avroh (descending) scale notes.

2. Define a Pattern(क्रम निर्धारित करें)

Enter a numeric sequence (e.g., 1234) or swaras (e.g., S G R) in the Pattern field.

3. Use the Swara Keyboard(स्वर कीबोर्रड़ का उपयोग करें)

Click the on-screen keyboard to insert swaras, flats, sharps, and diacritics into any input field.

4. Generate Alankar(अलंकार बनाएं)

Press ✨ Generate ✨ to produce the full ascending and descending sequences.

5. Play & Copy(परिणाम कॉपी करें)

Use ▶ Play to hear the sequence, or 📋 Copy Output to save it to clipboard.

Your generated Alankar will appear here.
