Research Portfolio & Blog

PhD Researcher specializing in Computational Linguistics. My work explores formal syntax, acoustic phonetics, and data-driven modeling for low-resource languages.

Core Research Areas

  • Computational Syntax - Formal approaches to syntactic analysis and parsing
  • Data Quality Estimation - Metrics and methods for corpus validation
  • Acoustic Phonetics - Speech signal processing and phonetic analysis
  • Odia NLP & Corpus Tools - Language resources for Odia language processing
  • Musicology & Prosody - Intersection of music theory and linguistic rhythm
  • Low-Resource Languages - NLP techniques for underrepresented languages
